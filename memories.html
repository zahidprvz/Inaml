My Story

It all began on January 24, 2023, when I first spoke to a girl who has since captured my heart. I had admired her from afar since my third semester, but it was only in my fifth semester that I mustered the courage to express my feelings.

About Inaml

Her name is Inaml, and she is pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Biochemistry at our university. In my third semester, I contemplated leaving my Computer Science degree due to various challenges I faced. However, one day, I caught a glimpse of her at the bus stop. At first, she seemed youthful and hurried, her presence striking a chord within me.

Dressed in a black abaya, her face was radiant with a hint of pink, complemented by a small sheen of perspiration on her forehead. I was captivated by her black hair and expressive eyes. Even though she was with friends, I felt an undeniable connection that drove me to learn more about her.

Memories

  • First Encounter: January 24, 2023 - I saw her at the busses area.
  • Second Encounter: Spotted her next to HBL Bank in the university.
  • Third Encounter: Noticed her in traditional attire during my fourth semester.
  • Investigation: Followed her to discover her department.
  • Letter: Attempted to initiate a conversation, by writing her a letter, but was not able to deliver it to her.
  • Chase: Followed her bus on a rainy day, witnessing her mother pick her up.
  • Last Day of my 4th sem: Saw her enjoying a slush on the final day of the semester but lost my chance to talk.
  • Semester Break: Spent the whole semester break thinking about her
  • 5th Sem: Found her sitting on grass, and just saw her for an hour or more, but was not confident enough to talk
  • 24 January 2023: Finally talked her, told her feelings, gave her all the poems i wrote up for her!
